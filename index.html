How Try-On Works

Without a Replicate key: Claude AI analyses your photo and generates a detailed style assessment with a visual preview.


With a Replicate key: the IDM-VTON model composites the garment directly onto your photo for a realistic overlay.


Note: Replicate requires publicly accessible image URLs. For local photos, the Claude AI fallback is used automatically.
